Difference between cosmetic surgery and plastic surgery
Cosmetic Surgery

Cosmetic surgery's main focus is to enhance appearance

    The key goals are:

  • Aesthetic appeal
  • Symmetry
  • Proportion
Plastic Surgery

Main focus is on repairing defects to reconstruct normal appearance and function

    Some common plastic surgeries performed:

  • Burn Repair Surgery
  • Scar revision surgery
  • Hand surgery

History of Plastic Surgery in South Korea
  • According to Confucian principle, “tampering with the body bestowed by one’s parents was a violation” and was discouraged
  • In August 1961, Dr. Jaeduck Yoo- a professional in plastic surgery in the US , founded the plastic surgery department at Yonsei University Severance Hospital
  • A decade ago cosmetic surgery was something that only rich people could afford however things have changed and it has become common now
  • 30 percent of Korean women aged 20 to 50 had surgical or nonsurgical cosmetic procedures
